About Us

Nifuate Foundation is an institute registered under the Ministry of Health, Community Development, Gender, Elderly and Children of the Government of Tanzania, Mainland, with Registration Number REG. OONGO/R/6925. Nifuate Foundation is a non-profit organization whose main aim is to connect individuals who have the heart and passion to help others but may not know how or where to begin. As the Swahili proverb says, ”Haba na haba hujaza kibaba.” At Nifuate Foundation, we believe in three core values: Unity, Love, and Humility, guided by our motto: ”Help 1 to Save 1000.”
